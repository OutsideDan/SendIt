# intent.md — climb-tracker

## What we're building

A rock climbing progress tracker app. Mira logs each climb and sees a chart showing her improvement over time.

## Who uses it

Mira. Possibly her dad, for comparison purposes (her idea, not his).

## How it works

**Logging a climb:**
- Tap "new climb"
- Enter: grade, number of attempts, whether she flashed or sent it

**Output:**
- A chart (bar graph or line) showing grades climbed over time
- Visualizes the trend — is the line going up?

## What "done" looks like

Mira can log a climb, and a chart updates to show her progress from her first session to now. The trend line goes up. Dad's line also goes up, but not as high — because Mira is better at climbing than him. (This is a fact, not a feature request.)

## Why we're building it

Fun, learning Claude Code, and having a real record of improvement — not just a feeling.

## Out of scope (for now)

- Photos or voice input
- Social features or comparing with more than one other person
- Automated syncing with a climbing gym's system
